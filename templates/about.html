About Bookit

Bookit is an application written by Jakob Calven with the intent of removing the guesswork in predicting hotel prices.

Using machine learning and hotel price data scraped from Booking.com, Bookit is able to predict how hotel prices are likely to change up to 6 months into the future. Currently, Bookit relies only on historical data to make predictions but a more sophisticated model is in the works that will enable Bookit to make predictions using current prices.

At the moment Bookit can only predict prices for Las Vegas, but San Francisco and New York will soon be added. Stay tuned!
